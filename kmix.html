kmix- audio mixer for the KDE Desktop Environment

This documentation has been upgraded to match V0.53

The mixer can control every mixer device, that OSS provides. Features are:

  • It uses /dev/mixer. You can give a parameter to use an other device, e.g call:   kmix /dev/mixer1
  • You have a single slider for mono devices.
  • You can have one or two sliders for stereo capable devices. Click with the right mouse button for a context menu to toggle this option.
  • You can mute mixer devices. Click with the right mouse button for a context menu to toggle this option.
  • The slider at the bottom is an overall balancing slider.
    • Drag it left to reduce the right channel volume of all devices
    • Drag it right to reduce the left channel volume of all devices
    • Click with the right mouse button for a context menu to restore center position, or to set balance to full left or right.
  • You can hide and show menu and tickmarks. Press CTRL-M and CTRL-T to do this

Thats it for today. Please mail every compilation problem and/or bug to Christian Esken, the author of kmix.

Sun and SGI mixer support is included, but should be regarded as PRE-alpha, so it is very likely not even compiling. Patches to get it running are very appreciated.
