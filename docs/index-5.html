5. Questions and answers

  • Q: I have switched the menu bar off. Do I have to hack the config file to get it back?

    A: No, just press Control-M

  • Q: When I leave kmix, and restart it, the mixer does not restore the "split" settings of the channels.

    A: This is intended. kmix determinates on startup, whether a "split" is neccesary, automatically. It splits the sliders, if both channels have different volume settings.

  • Q: The program does not save any options.

    A: The "Menu", "Tickmarks" and "Docking" option are saved automatically. The layout is saved manually in profiles.
