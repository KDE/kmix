1. Introduction

KMix is KDE's soundcard mixer program. It is thought as a convinient tool. Though small, it is full-featured. There are regulators for each device: One for mono devices, and as to your liking one or two for stereo devices. Single devices may be muted and the record source can be set.

Much fun with KDE's multimedia application wishes
